Jamie Corcoran

Neuroscience PhD student graduating in the winter. Investigated neuromolecular differences in stress and learning behavior through molecular imaging techniques and gene expression data. Looking to apply what I have learned in my PhD to real world industry applications. Passionate about both animal and human health..
